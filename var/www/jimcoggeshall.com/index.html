Meeting Rooms

Public Meeting Room - anyone with this link may join the meeting room.

Alternately, for a meeting that you want to be "reasonably private", click here to create a new room with an autogenerated URL. Anyone who knows the resulting autogenerated URL may join, but it's difficult for anyone just to guess it.

It's also possible to add a password and/or a waiting room to any existing meeting. Every meeting attendee is an admin. Camera and mic are switched off by default when joining a meeting, so be sure to enable them if you want to use them.

Whiteboards

Public Whiteboard - anyone with this link may view and edit the whiteboard.

In addition to the public whiteboard, every meeting room has its own unique whiteboard, which can be opened by selecting "Open shared document" from the in-meeting menu.
